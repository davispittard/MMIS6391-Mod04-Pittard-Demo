About Us

Praise be, citizens! Your beneficent ruler has deigned to provide you with a brief history of Davistopia Public Library. The library was founded in 2024 by the illustrious Davis Pittard, who sought to provide the citizens of Davistopia with a place of learning and enlightenment. Over 1 million books, periodicals, and encyclopedias are available for your perusal, so long as you have positive social credit!

Slake your intellectual cravings using our vast collection of reading material, which has been specially curated to avoid any unpatriotic or subversive content. Browse the web on our state-of-the-art computers, whose incredible processing power allows the viewing of a whole 5 websites! And don't forget to visit our gift shop, where you can purchase a variety of Davistopia-themed merchandise, including t-shirts, mugs, and keychains!
